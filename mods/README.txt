In this mod we are taken into a fantasy type of mod, the game itself runs like quake 4 and all you have to do is complete the game as normal only you will have a sword and elemental weapons among other things, you will start with full armor and there will be a level up system. 

you are also equipped with all weapons/abilities necessary these are binded to your keys and they go as follows:
	sword/gauntlet = 1
	icebeam = 4
	fireball = 8
	lightning = 9
	dark magic = 0
	summon = p
Items(the powerup potions are permanent as they are made to just make you more overpowered)
	HP potion = o(not zero, but the letter o)
	spellReacharge Potion=l
	speedup Potion(makes you faster)= k
	powerUp Potion(makes you stronger with weapons)=i
	highJumpUp potion(you can jump higher)=u
